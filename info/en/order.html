Photo order via Arachne

The photographic material is made available exclusively for scientific purposes and research. To order images for a publication, please note the templates of the photos:

  1. Scans of photographic negatives from the Research Archive for Ancient Sculpture (Forschungsarchiv für Antike Plastik) start with the following abbreviations: "FA", "FA-S" ("FA-Kae", "FA-Oe", „FA-HvH“ etc.), "PHG", "Fitt", "Mal", "Kruse", "Hannestad" or "Mercklin".

    These can be ordered via the contact form zunder the following conditions:

    • Shipment as file via e-mail attachment or provision of a printable scan for download, min. 300dpi-TIFF € 5 per image
    • Scans of not yet digitised negatives/copies on special request € 12 per image

    For bulk orders of 20 or more images and non-digitised negatives/copies, the processing time is usually between 5 and 8 weeks. Please take this into account when placing your order.

  2. Scans of photographic negatives of the German Archaeological Institute start with the abbreviations "D-DAI" or "INR".

    These can be ordered directly from the DAI. Please contact the following e- mail addresses depending on the abbreviation of the location used in the file name:

    • Athens: fotothek.athen@dainst.de
    • Berlin Zentrale: architekturreferat@dainst.de
    • Damascus: sekretariat.damaskus@dainst.de
    • Istanbul: otothek.istanbul@dainst.de
    • Cairo: sekretariat.kairo@dainst.de
    • Madrid: fotothek.madrid@dainst.de
    • Orient: fotothek.orient@dainst.de
    • Rome: fotothek.rom@dainst.de

  3. Scans from books (images with the photographic negative number "Repro") or from prints of other image archives or museums (e.g. images with the abbreviations "o.A." (without indication), "FM-PZ", "Marburg", "Oppenheim"or "Museum") cannot be ordered via Arachne. These images must be requested directly from the respective copyright holder (e.g. the corresponding museum or collection).

General provisions

  1. The prices refer to the pure processing costs. Even in the event that the requested photographic material is not used in publications, a paid fee cannot be refunded.
  2. In general, only the one-time right to use of the photographic copyright is assigned.
  3. The photographic material shall be delivered exclusively against advance payment. The payment modalities are indicated on the invoice. Credit cards will not be accepted.
  4. The sending of a specimen copy is requested.
  5. The respective permanent link of the corresponding record (e.g. http://arachne.dainst.org/entity/156877) and, if available, the image author must be cited as illustration credits.

If you have any questions or problems ordering photos, please contact the Arachne team at arachne-eingabe@uni-koeln.de
